About us
Episode 1

On a trip to try and free the planet Naboo, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Qui-Gon Jinn and Queen Padme Amidala crash on the planet Tatooine in order to repair their ship. There, they meet Anakin Skywalker, a young boy who is strong with the Force, a mysterious power also used by Kenobi and Jinn. They eventually repair their ship and leave, but Qui-Gon is killed by Darth Maul in the process. Kenobi decides to train Anakin as a Jedi, despite the warnings of those in charge.

Ep2

Anakin, now older, is tasked with being Padme’s bodyguard, along with Obi-Wan. They rescue her from an assassination, after which Obi-Wan discovers that a secret army of clones is being created. Anakin’s mother dies, and he becomes obsessed with trying to defeat death. An army of droids attempts to attack the republic, and Palpatine is given control over the clone army. Anakin secretly marries Padme.

The Clone Wars

Anakin gets a student, Ahsoka Tano, who fights alongside him, Obi-Wan, and the clone army. They face off against Maul again, as well as many other enemies. Eventually, Ahsoka is kicked out of the Jedi Order and goes into hiding.

Ep3

Padme reveals she is pregnant, and Anakin becomes worried that she will die. Palpatine tells Anakin that he has the ability to save her life if Anakin will turn to the dark side, which he does. Anakin and Obi-Wan fight on the fiery planet Mustafar, during which Obi-Wan cuts off all of Anakin’s limbs. Anakin is saved by Palpatine. Padme dies in childbirth, Obi-Wan goes into hiding, Palpatine becomes Emperor, and Anakin becomes Darth Vader.

Rebels

During the age of the Empire, a group aboard the Ghost decide to start rebelling against the Empire. In doing so, they form the beginnings of the Rebel Alliance, as well as facing off against enemies like the Inquisitors, Darth Vader, and Grand Admiral Thrawn.

Rogue One

Galen Erso, a scientist, is taken by the Empire to build a superweapon, the Death Star. However, he has secret rebel sympathies, sending his daughter Jyn to live with rebels, and secretly building a flaw into the Death Star. Many years later, Jyn forms a group to try and save her father, and though he dies in the process, she discovers that there is a flaw in the Death Star’s plans. She launches an operation to recover them, her and her team dying in the process, but the plans are eventually sent to Rebel Intelligence.

Ep4

Luke Skywalker is living on his aunt and uncle’s farm, but dreams of being a pilot. After he receives a droid with a message from a princess, he goes to visit Obi-Wan Kenobi. The group head offworld to rescue Leia, along with smugglers Han Solo and Chewbacca, and eventually save Leia. Luke joins the rebel alliance and blows up the Death Star by using the flaw in its plans.

Ep5

Luke heads to Dagobah in order to train with an ancient Jedi, Yoda. He leaves training early to try and save his friends, who have been ambushed in Cloud City by Vader. Luke fights Vader, but Han is captured and frozen in carbonite. Vader reveals that he is Luke’s father. Luke’s hand is cut off. Luke and Leia escape back to the rebels.

Ep6

The Empire decides to build a second Death Star to destroy the Rebels. Luke and Leia manage to rescue Han from being stuck in carbonite. Luke tells Leia that they are siblings, and then leaves to try and defeat Vader. Leia and Han head to Endor in order to disable the shield on the second Death Star, and as Luke fights both Vader and Palpatine, the second Death Star is also destroyed. Vader finally turns on Palpatine, sending him to his death, but also killing Vader. At a party back on Endor, Luke sees the ghosts of Anakin, Obi-Wan and Yoda watching over him.

Ep7

Many years later, Luke Skywalker has disappeared. A stormtrooper, Finn working for the First Order helps Poe Dameron, a rebel, to escape. Poe is separated from the group and Finn meets up with Rey, a scavenger. The steal Han Solo’s ship, eventually meeting with Han himself. Rey discovers she can use the Force, but is immediately captured by Kylo Ren, Han’s Son who idolises his grandfather, Vader. Han and Kylo meet and Kylo kills his father.
